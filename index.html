New Ad Campaign Structure · ₹10K/month

✦
The One Number to Watch
Once all fixes are live, your single north star is cost per purchase in Meta Ads Manager. Target: under ₹800 per order. Given your ₹2,950 AOV and ~50% gross margin, this gives you a healthy positive ROAS. If CPP stays above ₹1,500 after 7 days, rework the creative or audience — not the budget.
06

Organic Content System — Instagram

Instagram organic drives 67% of your revenue. This is your highest-ROI channel. Invest in it before spending more on ads.

✦ April Jaipur + Udaipur Trip — Your Biggest Content Unlock
Raw footage of block printers, indigo vats, and artisan hands on fabric is exactly what performs on Reels and justifies your ₹2,950 price point better than any ad copy. Plan to capture 15–20 short clips: process shots, artisan portraits, fabric textures, colour making. This feeds 6–8 weeks of content and builds the heritage story that makes Bimba Studio distinct from every generic kurta brand online.
